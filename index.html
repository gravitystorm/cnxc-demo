CNXC Snapshot demo

Welcome the the CNXC Snapshot demo. Please feel free to try out the system, and send your feedback to Andy or discuss on talk-gb.

More information is available on this project on the wiki

Two test areas are currently loaded

  • Nottingham
  • Cambridge

The background data is highlighted either orange (needs attention) or blue (already processed). Click the background features to select them. Ctrl+click (or cmd+click on a Mac keyboard) the relevant OSM feature to see a side-by-side comparison
